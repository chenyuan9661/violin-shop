About

With decades of experience in violin making, Auntie brings together traditional craftsmanship and a deep love for music. Every instrument is carefully shaped, voiced, and finished by hand — ensuring each violin has its own unique character and warm, resonant tone.

Whether you're a student looking for your first quality instrument or a professional seeking a distinctive voice, every violin that leaves our workshop is built to inspire.
